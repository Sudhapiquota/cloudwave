CloudWave Launches XCavate App for Salesforce Users to Backup, Search, and Retrieve Logs

TYSONS CORNER, VA June 3, 2020

CloudWave Inc., a proven Salesforce partner and a global leader in cloud strategy, big data consulting, and digital transformation technology announced today the launch of its XCavate App for Salesforce users.

Drive your business with the XCavate Mobile app. Download this simple plug-and-play app from App Store or Google Play Store. XCavate is an easy to use, reliable, enterprise-class cloud application that helps companies backup and retrieve Salesforce logs.

Do you currently back up Salesforce logs on all of your orgs? XCavate helps Salesforce users automatically archive historical logs. Typically, organizations in regulated industries such as financial services, healthcare, insurance, retail etc. are required to maintain logs for long periods of time. Also, they need to have a quick turnaround retrieval.

Business continuity is impacted in uncertain scenarios. Common scenarios encountered in organizations include: monitoring adoption of a new app, tracking performance of an existing app to identify opportunities for improvement, accidental removal of a record created earlier, etc. Therefore, it is critical to have a log backup system in place.

XCavate can unearth all of your logs. It is critical that your logs are accessible and auditable at a short notice to ensure business does not stop. XCavate helps Salesforce users backup logs, apply filters to identify logs, and use keyword search to spot logs. This saves enormous time and effort for Salesforce users that they would have spent manually otherwise. XCavate adds value by helping the user create charts to find patterns and insights as well as identify business opportunities.

“Enterprises today are forced to retain logs for compliance and other purposes, and they spend lots of time and money on implementing and maintaining custom solutions. XCavate is much more affordable and backs up all your logs for compliance, auditing, and tracking purposes with a very powerful search feature,” said Prabhakar Ramakrishnan, CEO, and founder of CloudWave Inc.

“Access all of your logs with our XCavate unified solution, optimized for any organization that uses Salesforce.”

For more information on XCavate, please visithttps://xcavate.us/.
