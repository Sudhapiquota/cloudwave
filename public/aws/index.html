Amazon Web Services for Cloud Computing

Amazon is a pioneer in cloud computing and they keep rolling out new versions of their AWS service at regular intervals. AWS product categories range from pure IT resources to databases for storage, analysis, artificial intelligence, game development, and mobile services to IoT solutions. AWS as a whole belongs to a category called Infrastructure as a Service in Cloud Computing (IaaS).

At CloudWave, our AWS Certified Technicians work with you to design, migrate, secure, leverage, and optimize your AWS cloud so you can focus on growing your business while we take care of the infrastructural responsibilities. We will help you mix and match our Managed Cloud Services so you only pay for services when you really need them.

Why CloudWave for AWS?

CloudWave is an official AWS consulting partner that can transform your business processes through the intelligent use of cloud-based applications.

Our approach is all about building the best-in-class cloud solution for our customers. We take an impartial perspective to help you determine which cloud computing platform is optimal for building your solution. We can assess your business needs and recommend whether AWS will be the right fit.

Benefits of AWS & CloudWave

COST REDUCTION

Your budget will likely impact your cloud migration strategy. We can periodically review your AWS configuration and look for ways to reduce costs and improve performance.

MIGRATION

Moving to AWS can be a daunting and exhausting task for those who have no experience. Using our best practices, we can reduce distractions and risks and help you execute a smooth transition to the cloud.

SECURITY & COMPLIANCE

We offer a solid combination of certified experts and management tools to protect your digital environment and the maintain the security and compliance of your data.

EXPERTISE

Finding and maintaining internal personnel with AWS experience is a challenge. We have a team of AWS technical consultants who can help you design and architect the AWS solution that best suits your business.

OPEN & FLEXIBLE

The AWS platform does not require one particular operating system and language. This means that you choose the programming model or development platform that best suits your business.

AGILE & EASY

AWS provides a global cloud infrastructure that allows you to innovate, experiment, and iterate quickly. And CloudWave has the experts to help you do it.

How We Work

Every project in the cloud is different. After evaluating your goals, we work with you to identify the cloud solution that best fits your business needs with a holistic approach that includes human resources, processes, and technology.
