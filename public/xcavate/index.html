XCavate

Dig Your Data in Salesforce

XCavate, developed by CloudWave, provides on-demand and scheduled daily backups of your Salesforce records with powerful advanced search tools to help you "dig your data" and find the records you need to recover faster than ever.

XCavate Key Features

Scheduled & On-Demand Backups

Customize the objects or event log types you need to back up and schedule for any day or time.

Security Compliance Tools

Maintain regulatory data compliance standards by keeping critical organizational data safe.

Permanent Retention of All Logs

Find out who did what within your Salesforce event logs, dating back as far as you need.

Powerful Relevant Search

Search by object type, file name, time period, and more to help you recover your records faster.

Protect Your Data

Do you have a simple way to back up your Salesforce data?

Let’s say an HR professional accidentally deletes an employee’s info, or the integration of a new accounting app overwrites critical financial data, or untested code from the IT department breaks your org and impacts records. When a Salesforce object record is deleted, it stays in the recycle bin for 15 days before it is deleted permanently. Retrieving it later is impossible. XCavate schedules daily backups of your system so that you can rest assured your data is accessible, no matter what happens.

Secure Your Event Logs

Can you easily track who did what within Salesforce?

Let’s say someone shared access to a folder with sensitive information externally, or someone posted an inappropriate message in Chatter and then deleted it later. Can you catch the culprit to address the issue? Normally, admins can only find detailed event logs on external websites for a few days after actions were taken.
XCavate allows you to back up event logs dating back as far as you need and instantly review them within the application.

Find Specific Records

Can you find old records in Salesforce with a few clicks?

Imagine looking for a needle in a haystack, or mining for precious gemstones with a regular garden spade. This analogy is what it feels like to hunt through records manually within Salesforce: time consuming and frustrating.
XCavate has advanced search capabilities powered by Elastic that make it easier than ever to locate and extract the record you’re looking for instantly.
