Job Requisition: Business Architect (IRS MBI Public Trust Clearance)

This position is for a Business Architect working for agencies like IRS, Treasury, and USDA. As a Business Architect, the candidate will be charged with multifaceted responsibilities to include collaborative efforts focused on development of a transformation vision, strategic planning, and execution of transformation-related priorities, influencing technology implementation and business operations optimization.

Basic Qualifications:

The candidate must have experience in performing the following activities.

  • Planning, coordinating, and implementing enterprise level programs or projects.

  • Conducting business analyses to identify alternative approaches to implementing new programs, products, and identifying enhancements to existing business processes.

  • Producing written reports and/or briefing materials that are suitable for presentation to the highest executive levels.
